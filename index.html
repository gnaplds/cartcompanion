❓ How to Use CartCompanion

📝 Creating Your Shopping List
Tap the "+ Add Item" button on the home page
Enter item details (name, category, price)
Optionally upload a photo or use the camera
Click "Add to Shopping List" to save
📱 Scanning Barcodes
Tap the center scan button in the bottom navigation
Point your camera at the product barcode
The app will recognize the product and show details
Add it directly to your shopping list or cart
Note: Scanning requires a Standard or Premium subscription
🛒 Managing Your Cart
Check items from your shopping list to add them to cart
Adjust quantities using the +/- buttons
Watch your budget bar to stay within limits
Tap "Complete Cart" when you're done shopping
💰 Budget Tracking
Your current budget is displayed at the top of the home page
The green bar shows how much budget you've used
Yellow warning appears when you're near your limit
Red alert shows when you exceed your budget
🤖 AI Recommendations
View "Most Bought Items" based on your purchase history
Check "You Might Also Need" for smart suggestions
Tap any recommendation to add it instantly
Note: AI features require a Premium subscription
📊 Viewing History & Analytics
Tap the "History" icon in the bottom navigation
View your weekly spending trends and patterns
See recent shopping trips and totals
Check monthly statistics and averages
⚙️ Profile & Settings
Tap the profile icon to access your account
Manage your subscription plan
Adjust shopping preferences and budget
Configure notifications and app settings
